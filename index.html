Project: Bank Statement Analysis for Mr. Jameson

This is my first personal project after completing a Data Analytics course. It analyzes finance data of Mr. Jameson to generate insights into the pattern of his monthly income and expenditure.

Tools Used

Microsoft Excel, Power BI

Key Findings

  • Expenditure within the given period exceeds the Total Income at ₦2,777,431.00 to ₦2,634,915.00
  • March and May had the highest expenditures: ₦688,551.25 and ₦487,397.50
  • Total Debit count: 558, Total Credit count: 118

Recommendation

Mr. Jameson should seek an additional stream of income to increase his earnings and maintain a balanced account.
